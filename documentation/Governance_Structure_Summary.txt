
Data Governance - Governance Structure - NYP Level:

Governance Structure:
- The ICT and Digitalisation Steering Committee (IDSC), chaired by the PCEO, guides the data governance strategy.
- The governance structure emphasizes leadership, accountability, and collaboration across teams, including CIO, CISO, CDO, and CNC Directorate.
- Two key committees, the DMC (Data Management Committee) and DAC (Data Application Committee), focus on compliance, data quality, classification, and data usage.
