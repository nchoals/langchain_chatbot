
Data Governance - Information Classification & Handling Policy:

Purpose:
- Defines policies for information asset classification and handling for NYP staff.

Information Classification:
- Information assets are categorized by security (e.g., Restricted, Confidential) and sensitivity (e.g., Sensitive High, Sensitive Normal).
- Proper classification ensures appropriate protection and control over unauthorized access or modification.

Handling Policy:
- Classified information should be securely handled, stored, and not left unattended.
- Restrictions are placed on hosting, storing, or processing classified information in non-government facilities or public cloud services.
