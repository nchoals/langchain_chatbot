
Data Governance - Data Management Committee (DMC):

Roles and Responsibilities:
- The Data Management Committee (DMC) consists of members like the CIO, CISO, CDO, and CNC Directorate, among others.
- They are responsible for harmonizing data efforts, focusing on compliance, data quality, and classification.
- They provide resolutions based on principles like SG-DRM standard adherence and minimizing duplication.
- Decisions consider cost, effort, and sustainability.
